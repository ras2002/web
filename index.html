Our Health Mission

We at Vaidyo act as bridge for the patients in rural areas
and the tier 1 & 2 doctors.
This can established through Telemedicine.

Our Health Vision

Vaidyo aims to reduce the death rate in rural areas due to
negligence and delay by involving urban medical specialists
who can monitor and come in touch with the patient to provide
necessary treatment as and when required.
